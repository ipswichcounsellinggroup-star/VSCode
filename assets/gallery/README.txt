Gallery Images
==============

This folder contains images for the gallery carousel displayed on the website.

Required Images:
- gallery1.jpg - Sample image 1
- gallery2.jpg - Sample image 2
- gallery3.jpg - Sample image 3
- gallery4.jpg - Sample image 4
- gallery5.jpg - Sample image 5
- gallery6.jpg - Sample image 6

Image Specifications:
- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Recommended size: 1200x800 pixels (landscape orientation)
- Aspect ratio: 3:2 or 16:9
- High quality, professional images showcasing your work, team, or office

Note: The carousel will display images in a full-width layout with
navigation controls and automatic sliding functionality.
